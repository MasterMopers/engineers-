The 2022 Davis Engineering Team Coding Competition will take place on September 17th, 2022 at the Yolo County Library Blanchard Room (315 E 14th St, Davis, CA 95616). The event will begin at 1:30pm and last until 2:30pm. The competition is open to students from grades 6-12, and will be separated into two divisions: grades 6-8 and grades 9-12. You will need to bring a computer and charger. Snacks and water will be provided, in addition to prizes for the winners of each division. If you have any questions, feel free to email us at davisengineeringteam@gmail.com, or text us through Remind.

Competition Format


  • You will have 2 hours to solve a pool of 20 problems ranging from easy to hard in difficulty. Each problem will vary in points, depending on their level. The challenge list is sorted from least to most challenging, which corresponds to the respective amount of points. There will be a leaderboard where you can view where each particpant is ranked. Good luck and have fun!

    • Prizes


  • Prize pools will be seperated into two divisions. The first division will include students from 6th to 8th grade. The second division will include students from 9th to 12th grade. First to third place will recieve giftcards and certificates of achievement.

    • Rules


  • You may use the internet but may not receive help from other people
  • The judges' rulings are final
  • You must bring your own computer
  • All solutions must programmatically determine the results; you cannot print out a solution that you determined by hand
  • You may only use C, C++, Java, Python 3, or Javascript
  • Tiebreakers will be determined by the number of invalid attempts submitted
  • The goal is to solve as many problems as possible in 3 hours. At the end, the participant with the most points wins
